The SCREAM team carried out their Senior Capstone Project
(Major Qualifying Project in WPI terminology) in 2018-2019. The team developed the
first prototype of the SCREAM device for laryngeal laser treatment.

Team members included Kevin O'Brien, Ben Mart, Cory Brolliar, and Zach Boyer.
